About HR Zone Calculation

The Karvonen formula uses your Heart Rate Reserve (Max HR − Resting HR) to give personalized zones.

Z1 (50–60% HRR) — Recovery  |  Z2 (60–70%) — Aerobic base  |  Z3 (70–80%) — Tempo  |  Z4 (80–90%) — Threshold  |  Z5 (90–100%) — VO2max
