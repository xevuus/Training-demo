About HR Zone Calculation

The Karvonen formula calculates your training zones based on Heart Rate Reserve (HRR = Max HR − Resting HR). Each zone is a percentage of HRR added to your resting HR, giving a more personalized result than fixed percentages of max HR.

Zone 1 (50–60% HRR) — Recovery
Zone 2 (60–70% HRR) — Aerobic base / fat burning
Zone 3 (70–80% HRR) — Aerobic / tempo
Zone 4 (80–90% HRR) — Threshold / lactate
Zone 5 (90–100% HRR) — VO2max / anaerobic
